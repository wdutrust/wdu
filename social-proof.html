1. Executive Summary

This document is provided primarily for transparency and blockchain explorer verification purposes.

WDU Trust Token (WDU) is an independently maintained blockchain-based digital token developed under the BEP-20 standard on the BNB Smart Chain.

The project is designed with a focus on transparency, verifiable smart contract behavior, and simplicity. WDU Trust does not represent an investment product and is not intended to generate financial returns.

The smart contract was deployed with a fixed total supply. No minting, burning, taxation, or administrative control mechanisms exist within the contract.

2. Project Overview

WDU Trust was created to provide a simple and transparent utility token for educational, experimental, and community-oriented blockchain use cases.

The project emphasizes open documentation, public verification, and consistency across all official resources, including the website, source code repository, and blockchain explorer data.
